Although Uranus is not of particular interest, it does have copious amounts of hydrogen, helium, and methane. Oddly, its rotation is sideways compared to its orbit. Perhaps close inspection could reveal something about system formation. It’s also one of two ice giants in the solar system, and just like the gas giants, it can’t be landed on.
