Possibly the most recognisable planet in the Solar System after Earth due to its beautiful rings and mysterious moon, Saturn is another immaterial gas giant. Its composition is mostly helium, and its rings may contain material wealth. However, unlike the Asteroid Belt, the objects in the rings are incredibly close together and thus dangerous.
