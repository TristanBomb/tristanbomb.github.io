Like Ceres, Pluto is less of a planet and more the king of a class of smaller bodies. Instead of the Asteroid Belt, Pluto policies the frozen Kuiper Belt. It is the last stop in the Solar System to resupply on solid, decently-sized ground before humanity sets out to the great abyss of interstellar space.
