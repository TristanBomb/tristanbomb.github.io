The most likely candidate for colonization after Mars, Titan contains the only significant atmosphere on a moon with thick orange clouds, as well as the only surface liquids outside of Earth. These lakes are not water, though - they are methane, which proves both a valuable fuel and a building block for life. Titan is the single most important location for scientific outposts in the Solar System, and it may even host alien life.
