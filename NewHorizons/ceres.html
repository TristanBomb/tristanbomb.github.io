Although it is far too small to be colonized (and certainly too small to be a planet), Ceres lies in a very strategically valuable place in the Solar System - smack in the middle of the Asteroid Belt, allowing for a good staging grounds for asteroid mining. Scientific and mining outposts may also be valuable here.
