Jupiter is an enormous gas giant - the largest planet in the solar system - that may be exploited for its large amounts of hydrogen. However, its crushing gravity and lack of solid ground means that interest comes mostly from its many moons. Maintaining a foothold here is also critical to expansion beyond.
